Rethink Astrology
=================

Rethink Astrology is an ODM (Object-Document Mapper) for RethinkDB,
closely modeled after SQLAlchemy's declarative extension. The goal is
to give you the benefits of an ORM, with the convenience and
flexibility of a schemaless database backend.
